Why Change the Name?

West Virginia is the only U.S. state with a directional name that stands alone—North Carolina has South Carolina, and North Dakota has South Dakota. It’s time for a reset. With your support, we can fund efforts to advocate for a name change and let the people decide on the new identity through blockchain-powered voting.
